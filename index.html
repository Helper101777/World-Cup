World Cup Rules

  • 2 Teams play against the same opponent for 2 consecutive rounds.
  • The 3rd team not mentioned acts as goalkeeper.
  • Each save, block, or protection = 1 point.
  • First goal scored = 1 point.
  • 5 saves = 1 point bonus.
  • Penalty save = 1 point.
  • Red and yellow cards can be given.
  • Standard football rules apply.
